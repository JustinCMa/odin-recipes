Lasagna

Description

This lasagna recipe takes a little work, but it is so satisfying and filling that it's worth it! This is how to make lasagna

Ingredients

  • Ground beef or sausage
  • Onion and garlic
  • Two cans of tomato sauce
  • Sugar
  • Spices and seasoning
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cheeses
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
  4. Layer the lasagna in the order of meat sauce, noodles, ricotta mixture, mozzarella slices, meat sauce, parmesan cheese.
  5. Cover with foil and bake at 375 degrees for 50 minutes. 25 minutes with foil and 25 minutes without foil.
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving.
